CGspam.cmd is used to invoke Spamassassin.
Go to www.spamassassin.org for info about it.
Version 2.42

Spamassassin will mostly work on OS/2, although
there is non portable code in the autowhitelist_DB
module.

Of course you need a working PERL.




CGATExit.cmd is used to invoke NORMAN AV
It could be used to invoke any program with
some simple modifications.

Some directories are hard coded in the scripts. You would do well
to adjust them to you environment. Also the directories must be
manually defined otherwise the scripts will probably fail.

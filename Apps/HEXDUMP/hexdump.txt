----------------------------------------------------------------------------
What HexDump does
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

HexDump prints a hex dump to standard output. This hex dump contains no characters below ASCII code 32, i.e. in particular no control characters that could cause, say, a line break.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------
How to use HexDump
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

See hexdump /h

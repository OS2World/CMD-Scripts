®MDBO¯®LS2¯®PT4¯®MDBO¯Remarks:®MDNM¯	The ®MDRV¯f®MDNM¯ and ®MDRV¯b®MDNM¯ command line parameters are letters designating the foreground and background screen colors, as shown in the table below.

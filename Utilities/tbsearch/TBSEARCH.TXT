TBSEARCH.CMD search for files, which match the given specification, in all
available directories on all available local and remote drives.

Start TBSEARCH.CMD without parameters, or with parameter /? or -?, to get
some usage information.

The syntax of TBSEARCH is :

   TBSEARCH <file>

Examples are :

   TBSEARCH "A HPFS file"
   TBSEARCH AUTOEXEC.BAT
   TBSEARCH *.SYS
   TBSEARCH CON*
   TBSEARCH /?
   TBSEARCH ?
